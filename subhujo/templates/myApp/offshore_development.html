Our ODC Models:

We offer ODC services in two models.

Contractor ODC Model

  • Business can outsource the entire project to us. We will be responsible for managing the project team and delivering the project successfully.

  • We provide the necessary infrastructure, resources, and expertise to complete the projects within the agreed-upon timeline and budget.

  • Leverage the technology skills and capabilities of Subhujo while maintaining flexibility and control over your project execution.

  • Our skilled development teams can quickly adapt to your evolving requirements.

  • Stay agile and achieve your goals with fixed costs or hourly rates.

Customer ODC Model

  • Businesses can set up dedicated development centers at Subhujo's offices.


  • Our offshore development center acts as an extension of our customer's existing team, enabling them to work collaboratively on software development projects.

  • We offer advantages like highly skilled technical resources, enhanced control over the development process and the ability to scale the team based on project requirements.

  • Customers gain more control and flexibility over the development process, team structure, and resource allocation.
