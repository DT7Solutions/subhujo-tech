01

Analysis the learning Needs

This step involves assessing the skills, knowledge, and competencies required by employees to perform their roles effectively through performance evaluations, skills gap analysis, surveys, or feedback from managers and employees.

02

Desgin the training

The training program addresses the identified needs and achieves the set objectives by determining the training content, methodologies, instructional strategies, and the program's overall structure.

03

Deliver the training

The training program is delivered to the participants using various methods such as in-person instructor-led sessions, virtual classrooms, e-learning platforms, workshops, or on-the-job training based on the nature of the content, learner preferences, and logistical considerations.

04

Evaluate the training

The overall effectiveness of the training program is evaluated by analyzing participant feedback, assessing changes in behaviour or performance, and measuring the impact on key performance indicators or organizational goals helps identify strengths, areas for improvement, and the return on investment for the training initiative.
